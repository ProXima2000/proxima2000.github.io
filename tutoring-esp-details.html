How We’ll Work Together

Every learner is different – and so is every course I offer. Here’s how we’ll make sure your English learning fits you perfectly:

Initial Assessment

We’ll begin with a short, informal test to check your current English level – speaking, listening, reading and writing – depending on your needs.

Subject Knowledge Check

I’ll also ask a few questions or set a quick task to see how familiar you are with the topic you’ve chosen (e.g. Python, photography, music theory). You don’t need to be an expert – just show me where you’re starting from.

Personalised Course Plan

Based on both assessments, I’ll design a course tailored to your level – in both English and subject knowledge. That means:

  • No time wasted on what you already know
  • Step-by-step language and skills that match your pace
  • Materials and activities suited to your goals

Flexible, Ongoing Support

As we work together, I’ll adapt the course if your needs change. I’ll check your progress regularly and adjust our focus to keep you moving forward with confidence.

This process ensures that you learn exactly what you need, when you need it, without unnecessary repetition or confusion – just practical, purposeful English that fits your world.
