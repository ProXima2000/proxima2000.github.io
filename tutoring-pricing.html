Online Tutoring: Pricing

Contact Me

I offer personalised online tutoring sessions tailored to the learner’s needs, goals, and schedule. Below you will find information about my pricing structure and what is included in each session.

Session Length and Rates

  • 15-minute free phone or chat consultation (with a parent) to discuss your goals and learning needs
  • 30-minute complimentary introductory session to get to know the student, explore interests, and understand their learning style
  • 60-minute lesson session: £40
  • 45-minute lesson session: £30 (ideal for younger learners or focused practice)
  • package of 5 lesson sessions (60 min each): £190 (save £10)

Payment Methods

  • Payments are accepted via PayPal or bank transfer.
  • Payment is required before the first lesson or before starting a package.
  • Invoices and payment details will be provided upon booking.

Cancellation Policy

  • Please provide at least 24 hours’ notice (text or email) to reschedule or cancel a lesson.
  • Late cancellations (less than 24 hours) or no-shows will be charged in full.
  • If you need to reschedule, I will do my best to accommodate your preferred time.

Booking and Consultation

Get in touch for a free 15-minute consultation to discuss your child’s needs and the best tutoring plan. Contact me via WhatsApp, Telegram or my blog form.
