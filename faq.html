Frequently Asked Questions

Contact Me

  1. How can I contact you?

    Follow the link "Contact me"

  2. How do I book your services?

    Bookings are made by contacting me directly. I mostly work with people I know personally, so if you’re here, I’m assuming you already have my contact details.

  3. What payment methods do you accept?

    I accept payments via PayPal and bank transfer. If you prefer another method, just let me know and we can discuss options.

  4. How long does it usually take to get a response from you?

    I typically respond within 24 hours on business days.

  5. Are you on Fiverr, LinkedIn or other platforms?

    I do have profiles on Fiverr, LinkedIn, and some other social media platforms — but I don’t use them for work. The commission fees are high, which pushes up the cost for clients, and the visibility on those platforms is quite limited. Since I mainly work with people I know offline, I prefer to discuss everything directly and agree on terms in person.

  6. Do you handle everything yourself when creating a website?

    I personally prepare website content and take care of the HTML and CSS coding. For JavaScript and other programming tasks, I collaborate with a trusted developer. I also often work with a graphic designer to create visuals.

  7. Do you offer refunds or satisfaction guarantees?

    Customer satisfaction is important to me. Refund policies depend on the service and specific circumstances.

  8. How do you ensure confidentiality and data privacy?

    All client information is kept strictly confidential and handled according to data protection regulations.

  9. Can I request a custom package or service?

    Yes! I’m happy to discuss custom requests and tailor my services to your needs.
